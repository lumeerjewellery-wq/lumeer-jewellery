How to measure your wrist, hand and ring size for the perfect fit.
Wrist Size
How To Measure
Your Wrist
1
Wrap
Wrap a string around your wrist to your desired fit.
2
Measure
Lay the string flat and measure it with a ruler.
3
Record & DM Us
Write down the length and DM us for more info.
Hand / Bracelet Size
How To Measure
Your Hand
1
Position
Position your hand as if you were wearing a bracelet. Tuck your thumb into your palm.
2
Measure
Measure the exact circumference of your hand by passing the string around your knuckles.
3
Size Up
We recommend that you size up for a more comfortable fit.
Ring Size
How To Measure
Your Ring Size
1
Wrap
Wrap a string around your finger at the desired fit.
2
Mark
Mark where the string overlaps.
3
Measure
Measure the length up to the mark using a ruler.
4
Find Your Size
Use your measurement and the chart below to find your ideal ring fit.
