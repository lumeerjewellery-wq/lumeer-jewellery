Who We Are
Our Story

LUMeeR Jewellery bridges elegance and accessibility. Our pieces are affordable yet refined — making it possible for anyone who loves jewellery to wear something beautiful without overspending.

Each piece is designed to enhance, not overshadow — the natural beauty of the wearer, allowing everyone to shine in their own unique way.

We believe jewellery deserves its own space. That's why we created a focused destination where you can explore gold, silver, and everything in between.

From classic gold-plated pieces and durable stainless steel, to rose gold finishes, gold vermeil on sterling silver, and pure 925 silver — everything is in one place, for you.

From the Founder
A Note From Us

Jewellery, to us, has always been about this moment.

Not forever. Not perfection. Just the feeling of putting something on and thinking — yes, this is exactly what we wanted today.

We started Lumeer because we believe jewellery is fashion. It's self-expression. There are no rules about what "goes together" or what's "appropriate" — if it makes you feel beautiful, that's the only opinion that matters.

Will pieces fade over time? Yes. Will you fall out of love with a style eventually? Probably. But that's not a flaw — that's just life. What matters is the joy it brings you right now, the way it completes an outfit, the way it makes you feel seen.

That's also why we keep Lumeer affordable — not because quality doesn't matter, but because we want you to have options. To mix, to match, to experiment. To grab that delicate necklace for Monday, a bold chain for Friday, and something sparkly just because. Fashion is more fun when you're not choosing between pieces — when you can have them all.

Jewellery doesn't need to last forever to mean something. It just needs to make today feel a little more like you.
